Beethoven's Mom

  • High Concept: A puzzle where you match the sheet music to the song
  • Player Experience Goals: Accomplishment
  • Genre: Puzzle/point and click/card game
  • Game play: flame button - plays the song you are trying to recreate blue button - plays your current attempt at recreating it (doesn't currently work. pretty press animation, though) Green check button - checks your work, plays awesome version of problem song if you got it right. Currently, you always get it right. flame violin and piano - changes the playback instrument for the flame and blue buttons. Also the measures, though I haven't implemented that yet. Measures - drag and drop into the order you think the song is. after clicking, you can use the WASD keys to flip and invert the measures - the measures are not necessarily in the correct orientation or order.
  • Prototype goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
  • Features: multiple playback instruments random orientation as well as order of puzzle pieces Train your ear! learn how to read rhythms in sheet music Awesome classical music
  • Setting/Premise: none
  • Story: none
  • Target Audience:
  • Play time: Dependent on player knowledge, song being used for the puzzle, possible difficulty levels. this demo shouldn't take long.
  • Strategies: keep track of pitch going up or down use the playback functions to check your work often difficult choral things - listen to the part that moves, try and find the rhythm, not just the notes.
  • Prototype Assets: I made nearly everything from scratch. the background is from http://becuo.com/cool-music-background-hd. the awesome version of twinkle twinkle little star is performed by Stefano Ligoratti, though I made the beginning and merged them in audacity Creative Commons Attribution 3.0, posted on IMSLP, the International Music Score Library.
